PicTree is a easy to use tree identification application. Start by looking at the tree and determine if it is deciduous or coniferous. If you are unable to do that please read the help on those topics. You can also just click the browse tree option and look for the tree you are trying to id.


After you pick the type you will see a part or parts of the tree to look at under the small PicTree. For example if you pick deciduous you will see leaves. Then two choices with a small description above the picture. Look at the part of the tree in this case the leaves. Are they opposite or whorled or are they alternate. Again if you dont know a term or need more help pick the question mark. Some options have multiple parts of the tree to look at for example Fruit/Number Leaflets/Type/ Twig Film. Examine the tree and pick the best choice.


Once you get to a tree you will see pictures and you can scroll down and left and right to see more.


You will see a flag next to the question mark. This can be used to take you to the home screen.
